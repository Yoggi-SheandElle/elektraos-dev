FAQ

Common questions.

Is the audit really free?

The initial audit is free for first-time domains. We do this so SMBs can see the report before deciding whether to engage. There's no card capture, no auto-subscription.

How long until I hear back?

Auto-confirmation within minutes. Full report and walkthrough booking within five working days from the day we receive your request.

Do you audit sites in Arabic, French or Danish?

Yes. Multilingual entity coherence is a core pillar. We currently audit in DK, EN, FR and AR. Other languages on request.

Will you write to my site?

No. The audit is read-only. We never write to your CMS, never deploy a tag, never request elevated access. We crawl public URLs only.

What happens to my data after the audit?

We keep the report on file for our records. Raw crawl data is deleted after 30 days unless you've opted into a managed plan. GDPR Article 17 erasure on request.

Can I share the report with my team?

Yes. The PDF is fully shareable. The HTML version lives on a private URL with a 90-day expiry that you can extend.
