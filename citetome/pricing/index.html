FAQ

Billing & scope.

How is Citetome different from an SEO agency?

Agencies bill hours. We bill outcomes against a fixed deliverable list. Every plan has a signed governance pact - your scope is the contract, not a vague monthly retainer.

Do you cover both AEO and GEO?

Yes. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) covers HTML and structured-data legibility for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews and Claude. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) tracks actual citation behaviour and entity attribution. Audit Pro and all managed plans include both.

What if my site is in French, Arabic or Danish?

Citetome was built around customers across Europe, the UK and Morocco. Multilingual entity coherence (hreflang reciprocity, language-variant entity IDs) is part of Audit Pro and Standard upwards. We currently audit in DK, EN, FR and AR; other languages on request.

Do you auto-publish changes?

No. Architectural rule. Proposals land in an approval queue. A human approves. Your own infrastructure pulls approved changes. We never write to your CMS without explicit sign-off.

What happens to my data if I leave?

Your governance log, entity catalogue and audit history are exportable as JSON on departure. GDPR Article 17 erasure is supported on demand. We retain hashed actor IDs for compliance audit trail only.

Do you do custom scopes?

For 3+ domains, regulated verticals (legal, finance, healthtech) or franchise networks, write to hello@citetome.com. We quote per-portfolio with a fixed scope sheet, not hourly.
