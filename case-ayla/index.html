// The Brief

A luxury brand with no digital infrastructure

A luxury jewelry brand needed a complete digital presence. No existing website. No SEO foundation. No automated customer support. No content strategy. The requirement was straightforward: build everything from zero. The site shipped and is live at aylamaison.com.

The brand positioned itself at the intersection of craftsmanship and modern luxury. Every digital touchpoint needed to reflect that standard, from page load speed to the tone of the AI chatbot.

// What Was Built

Four systems. One cohesive digital presence.

E-Commerce Website

Responsive, conversion-optimized storefront. Clean product pages, streamlined checkout flow, and performance tuned for mobile-first luxury browsing.

SEO Strategy

Technical audit, keyword targeting, and content optimization. Built to compound organic visibility over time rather than rely solely on paid acquisition.

AI Chatbot

Handles customer inquiries, captures leads, and supports multilingual conversations. Trained on brand voice and product catalog for consistent responses.

Content Pipeline

Automated social media and blog publishing. Scheduled content delivery across platforms with brand-consistent visuals and copy.

// June 2026 Upgrade

Rebuilt for the AI era, design untouched

In June 2026 the live site went through a Citetome AI-visibility audit. The verdict: readable by AI assistants, but not transactable by AI agents. The fixes shipped the same day - structured data, machine-readable feeds, and direct ordering - without changing a pixel of the luxury design layer.

Structured Data

JSON-LD Organization, JewelryStore, and WebSite nodes, plus Product and Offer nodes with MAD prices on the homepage.

Machine-Readable Feed

products.json feed plus llms.txt and AI crawler directives in robots.txt, so agents can read the catalog without scraping the DOM.

WhatsApp Ordering

Customers order directly over WhatsApp - the channel the brand's market already lives on.

Owner Self-Publish Portal

A manage page that commits content through the GitHub API via api.citetome.com. The owner updates products without touching code.
