Fair pricing . 10 markets . 4 scale tiers

One stack, priced for where you are and what you make.

We price the same work differently by market, because a pound does not go the same distance in Accra as it does in Copenhagen. On top of that, we price by your scale: a 3-person seed operator does not pay like a 40-person established one. A 40 percent equity discount stacks on top for underrepresented founders, women-first teams, social enterprises, student-led ventures, and operators in post-conflict regions.

01 . Market PPP factor

Every country has a purchasing-power factor against the UK anchor. DK 1.15, EU 1.0, MA 0.35, GH 0.22. Not a discount, a local floor.

02 . Scale multiplier

Seed 0.35, Growth 0.65, Scale 1.0, Established 1.30. Based on headcount and annual revenue, self-declared and verified once at onboarding.

03 . Equity discount

Minus 40 percent stacks on top. Women-first teams, underrepresented founders, social enterprises, student-led ventures, post-conflict regions.

04 . Transparent rail

Every tier is published at /smb/pricing/rates.json. Loomgraph and locale sites pull the same numbers. No hidden local markup.
