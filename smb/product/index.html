ElektraOS for SMB . Pillar 03
03 . Commerce and Product

Agent-native storefronts, modern checkout, mobile.

Ship a storefront AI assistants can actually parse and quote, with Schema.org coverage, feed.json, an approval queue and a mobile-grade checkout. Lifted straight from MealShift's Apr 22 launch pattern, scoped into a package an SMB can buy.

What is included

Everything an agent needs to find your catalogue, quote accurately, and hand off to a checkout a human finishes on a phone. No stack migration required; the package plugs into the CMS the team already runs.

Layer . Discovery

Schema.org and feed.json

Product, Offer, Organization, Review, FAQ schemas. Machine-readable feed.json at a stable URL so agents can crawl without JavaScript rendering.

Layer . AEO

llms.txt and ai-robots.txt

Per-store llms.txt declaring what agents are allowed, what endpoints to hit, and where the catalogue lives. Matches the Loomgraph Society pattern.

Layer . Plugins

WooCommerce, Shopify, Wix

Native plugins for the three CMS stacks 80 percent of UK SMBs run. Plugin pushes schema, feed, llms.txt. No front-end rewrite needed.

Layer . Approval

Agent approval queue

Agents propose; humans approve. Orders, price changes, content edits queue for sign-off. Audit log per approval.

Layer . Checkout

Mobile-grade checkout

Stripe, Klarna, Apple Pay, Google Pay. Optimised for thumb-first flows. Abandoned-cart agent hooks into the ops pillar.

Layer . Analytics

Commerce signals to the data pillar

Every purchase, abandonment, agent-driven session lands in the data warehouse. Dashboards attribute revenue by source.

First 90 days

Three months from whatever storefront exists today to an agent-native storefront that quotes accurately and checks out on mobile.

Day 0 to 30

Audit and AEO foundation

  • Storefront audit against Schema.org coverage
  • Deploy feed.json, llms.txt, ai-robots.txt
  • Pick the plugin track: Woo, Shopify or Wix
  • Crawl verified from a live agent
Day 31 to 60

Approval queue and checkout

  • Approval queue wired to Slack or email
  • Checkout upgrade for mobile weight classes
  • Payment coverage: Stripe plus two local methods
  • First agent-driven order end to end
Day 61 to 90

Launch and compound

  • Abandoned-cart agent live
  • Cross-link with Loomgraph Society peers
  • Commerce signals flowing into data pillar
  • Launch note plus case-study draft

Proof this is not greenfield

The pattern is live on the platform that informs this pillar. SMB package reuses the exact surface.
