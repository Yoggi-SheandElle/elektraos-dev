Revenue split

70 / 30

Hub lead keeps 70 percent of gross local revenue. 30 percent feeds the shared runtime, the equity pool that subsidises the 40 percent equity discount, and quarterly compliance review. Reviewed annually. Any change requires 6 months' notice to existing hub leads.

Minimum commitment

90 days

12-month minimum to commit. Exit on 90 days' notice. No non-compete, no IP transfer, no claw-back on revenue already earned. You own your local brand and your local client relationships.

What we provide, what you provide

  1. We provide the runtime. ElektraOS (3,852-line core, 49 agents, 566 endpoints), Loomgraph (Fly.io, 12 routers, public join), analytics (Umami on Fly.io), and every locale page infrastructure.
  2. We provide the compliance work. EU AI Act readiness, GDPR reconciliation, data residency per market, audit trail. Documented in the Governance Pact.
  3. We provide brand and sales material. /smb/ in your locale, /pricing/ in your currency, /compliance/ pages, case study templates, Loomgraph Society seat.
  4. You provide local reach. Outreach, community, first 3 operators in 90 days, language authenticity, local events.
  5. You provide local regulatory sense. You flag when the Moroccan Data Protection Law 09-08, the Ghana DPA 2012, or a specific EU member-state rule needs a product change.
  6. You publish a quarterly hub report. Revenue, operator count, equity-tier take-up, local feedback. Goes on /log/ publicly.
