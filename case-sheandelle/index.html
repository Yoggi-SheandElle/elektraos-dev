Brand + Website

She&Elle of Morocco

Brand identity and digital presence for a Moroccan-Scandinavian lifestyle brand.

// The Vision

Two cultures. One cohesive identity.

A brand that merges Moroccan warmth with Scandinavian minimalism. Content spanning food, lifestyle, and cultural storytelling. The challenge was building a cohesive digital identity that honored both design traditions without diluting either.

The aesthetic needed to feel simultaneously warm and restrained. Rich textures and earthy tones from Moroccan craft traditions, balanced against the clean geometry and white space of Nordic design. Every element, from typography to color palette, required deliberate calibration.

// What Was Built

A complete brand system and digital home

WordPress Website

Custom GeneratePress theme build. Lightweight, fast-loading, and designed for long-form content with editorial-quality layouts.

Brand Identity

Color palette, typography system, and visual language. A deliberate fusion of warm Moroccan tones and clean Scandinavian structure.

Content Strategy

Cross-platform publishing framework covering food, lifestyle, and cultural storytelling. Structured for consistency and growth.

Social Automation

Automated social media pipeline. Scheduled publishing, content repurposing, and platform-specific formatting.

Cultural Design Balance

Visual system that blends two distinct aesthetic traditions into a singular, recognizable brand. Warm but minimal. Rich but clean.
